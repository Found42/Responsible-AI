Thinking Like a Scientist

Astrophysicist Neil deGrasse Tyson says that science isn't just a collection of facts‚Äîit's a way of looking at the world. It's about evidence.

Question Authority

Just because a "smart" computer (AI) said it, doesn't mean it's true. AI can "hallucinate" (make things up).

Demand Evidence

Don't accept a claim until you can verify where it came from. What is the source?

Triangulate

Like GPS needs satellites to find you, you need multiple sources to find the truth.
